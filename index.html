About Bhubon Pegu

Bhubon Pegu, age 54, is an Indian politician from Assam currently serving as a Member of the Assam Legislative Assembly (MLA) for the Jonai constituency. He was elected in 2006 and 2016 from Jonai, and joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) in 2020. He is a three-time Member of the Assam Legislative Assembly and focuses on grassroots development, education, connectivity, and social welfare.

With decades of public service and community engagement, Bhubon Pegu continues to work for sustainable economic growth and inclusive policies for Jonai and Assam.
